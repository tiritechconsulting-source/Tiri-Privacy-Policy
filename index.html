Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 6, 2025

This privacy policy explains how Tiri App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Camera Access

  • Purpose: To scan QR codes for quick access to service requests and user verification
  • Usage: Camera is used only for QR code scanning; no photos or videos are captured or stored
  • Storage: QR code data is processed locally on your device and not stored on our servers

Location Data

  • Purpose: To show nearby help requests and connect users in the same area
  • Usage: Location is used to match users with relevant community requests
  • Storage: Location data is stored temporarily and not shared with third parties

Personal Information

  • Data Collected: Name, email address, phone number (optional)
  • Purpose: Account creation and communication between users
  • Usage: To facilitate the community help network

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our service
  • To notify you about requests in your area
  • To enable communication between community members
  • To improve user experience and app functionality

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained until account deletion is requested
  • Service requests: Retained as long as the request is active
  • Location data: Stored temporarily for matching purposes only
  • QR code scans: Processed locally and not stored

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of location tracking (may limit app functionality)
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Maps: For location services and mapping
  • Firebase: For authentication and cloud services

These services have their own privacy policies governing the use of your information.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for significant changes

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@tiriapp.com
  • Website: https://tiriapp.com
