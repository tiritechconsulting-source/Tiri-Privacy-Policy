Account Deletion

Last updated: November 15, 2025

Tiri App respects your privacy and your right to control your personal data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your account and associated data.

Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your Tiri App account and all associated personal data, please contact us using one of the following methods:

  • Email: privacy@tiriapp.com
  • Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Include in Your Request: Your account email address or phone number

Steps to Delete Your Account

Option 1: Email Request (Recommended)

  1. Send an email to privacy@tiriapp.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request"
  2. Include your registered email address or phone number in the email body
  3. We will verify your identity within 3-5 business days
  4. Upon verification, your account and data will be scheduled for deletion
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete

Option 2: In-App Deletion

  1. Open the Tiri App on your device
  2. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  3. Enter your password to confirm your identity
  4. Review the information about what will be deleted
  5. Tap "Confirm Deletion" to proceed
  6. Your account will be deleted immediately and you will be logged out

Data Deleted Upon Account Deletion

When you request account deletion, the following personal data is permanently deleted:

  • Your account profile information (name, email, phone number)
  • Your location history and location data
  • All service requests you have created
  • All service requests you have responded to
  • Your communication history and messages
  • Your account preferences and settings
  • Your rating and review history

Data Retained After Account Deletion

The following data may be retained for legitimate business and legal reasons:

  • Transaction Records: Anonymized transaction data may be retained for accounting and legal compliance (up to 7 years)
  • Abuse/Safety Reports: If your account was involved in reported safety or policy violations, records may be retained for security purposes
  • Legal Obligations: Data required by law (tax records, dispute resolution, etc.) may be retained as required
  • Backup Data: Backups may be retained for up to 90 days, after which all data is permanently destroyed

Data Deletion Timeline

  • Email Requests: Account deletion within 10-15 business days after identity verification
  • In-App Deletion: Account deletion within 24 hours
  • Complete Data Removal: All personal data permanently deleted within 90 days (after backup purge)

Important Information

Before You Delete Your Account

Please note that account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted:

  • You will not be able to access the Tiri App with that account
  • Any active service requests will be canceled
  • You will not receive updates about assistance you requested or offered
  • Your profile and contributions to the community will no longer be visible

Alternative Options

If you simply want to take a break from using Tiri App, consider these alternatives to deletion:

  • Temporarily Deactivate Account: Pause your account without losing data
  • Disable Notifications: Turn off app notifications in Settings
  • Update Privacy Settings: Adjust location sharing and visibility preferences

Questions or Issues?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process, encounter issues, or need assistance, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@tiriapp.com
  • Website: https://tiriapp.com

We will respond to your inquiry within 2-3 business days.
