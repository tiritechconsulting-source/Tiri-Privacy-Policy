Safety Standards

Last updated: December 4, 2025

Tiri App is committed to the safety and protection of all users, with particular emphasis on preventing Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE). This document outlines our comprehensive safety standards and measures.

Our Commitment to Child Safety

We take child safety extremely seriously. The Tiri App platform is designed to be a trusted community tool, and we maintain rigorous standards to protect minors from exploitation and abuse.

Content Moderation & CSAE Prevention

Automated Detection Systems

  • Implementation of industry-standard PhotoDNA technology for detection of known CSAE material
  • Automated scanning of user-uploaded content and shared media
  • Machine learning algorithms to identify suspicious activity patterns
  • Real-time monitoring for grooming behavior and exploitation indicators

Manual Review & Moderation

  • Dedicated trust and safety team monitoring reported content
  • Rapid response protocol for CSAE-related reports (within 24 hours)
  • Comprehensive review of flagged user accounts and communications
  • Continuous training for moderators on CSAE indicators and response procedures

User Verification

  • Identity verification for users engaged in sensitive activities
  • Age verification mechanisms to prevent minors from accessing restricted features
  • Phone number verification to reduce anonymous abuse accounts
  • Behavioral analysis to detect account takeovers and unauthorized access

Reporting & Response Procedures

User Reporting Tools

  • One-click reporting system for suspected CSAE content
  • Anonymous reporting options to protect reporters
  • Detailed report categorization for rapid triage
  • Multi-language support for international users

Law Enforcement Cooperation

  • Direct partnerships with National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • Compliance with CyberTipline reporting requirements
  • Rapid disclosure of evidence to law enforcement upon legal request
  • Full cooperation with investigations into CSAE incidents
  • Preservation of evidence and digital forensics support

Account Suspension & Termination

  • Immediate suspension of accounts engaged in CSAE-related activities
  • Permanent ban for users with confirmed CSAE involvement
  • Device-level restrictions to prevent account circumvention
  • Reporting of confirmed offenders to law enforcement

User Education & Awareness

Safety Resources

  • In-app safety tips and best practices for users
  • Educational content about online grooming and manipulation tactics
  • Resources for parents and guardians about monitoring and protection
  • Links to external support organizations (NCMEC, Internet Watch Foundation, etc.)

Parental Controls

  • Optional parental oversight features for minor accounts
  • Privacy-respecting monitoring with minor consent notifications
  • Ability for parents to view activity summaries and contact logs
  • Guidance for parents on setting healthy usage boundaries

Data Protection & Privacy

Secure Infrastructure

  • End-to-end encryption for private communications
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive user data
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing
  • GDPR and CCPA compliance for user data protection

Data Minimization

  • Collection of only necessary user information
  • Automatic deletion of sensitive data after retention period expires
  • Limited third-party sharing of user information
  • Strict access controls for employees handling user data

Third-Party Safety Services

  • PhotoDNA: For detection of known child exploitation material
  • National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC): CyberTipline partnership and reporting
  • Internet Watch Foundation: Collaboration on CSAE content removal
  • Safety organizations: Industry partnerships for best practice development

Report CSAE Content

If you encounter child sexual abuse or exploitation material on our platform, please report it immediately:

  • In-App: Use the report button within the application
  • NCMEC CyberTipline: https://cybertipline.org
  • Email: tiritechconsulting@gmail.com

Compliance & Standards

Tiri App adheres to the following safety standards and frameworks:

  • Internet Watch Foundation Code of Practice: Commitment to preventing CSAE content
  • NCMEC Standards: CyberTipline reporting requirements and protocols
  • UN Convention on the Rights of the Child: Protection of children's rights and safety online
  • GDPR & CCPA: User privacy and data protection regulations
  • COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act): Protection of users under 13 (where applicable)

Transparency & Accountability

We are committed to transparency in our safety efforts:

  • Regular publication of safety reports and statistics
  • Independent audits of our safety systems and procedures
  • Open feedback channels for safety concerns and suggestions
  • Annual review and update of safety standards
  • Clear documentation of our content moderation policies
